Welcome to Our Culture!

Welcome to NuStars Haitian Cuisine, a hub for many NYC residents to enjoy the diverse selection of Haiti's own delicious dishes. Ranging from the superb selection of rices, herbs, meats and fish, it'll be as if you're eating straight from the country itself, making our Haitian customers feel right at home.

Our founders, Jean Noel and Victoire Lafleur, a Haitian immigrant couple sacrificed years to add the NYC melting pot with authentic Haitian dishes.
